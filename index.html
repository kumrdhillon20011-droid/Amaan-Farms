Our Story

Built on a belief
in real food.

We started Amaan Farms because we couldn't find milk we truly trusted — for ourselves, for our children, for the people we love. The kind of milk our grandparents drank: from animals they knew, on land they could see, without the industrial chain in between.

Our farm sits near Amritsar International Airport, home to indigenous desi breeds whose A2 milk has nourished Indian families for centuries. We milk at dawn. We bottle by hand. We deliver before the city wakes up.

We believe that what you eat is a statement about what you value. We built Amaan Farms for people who take that seriously.

Why A2 Milk

The science of
better milk.

🧬

A2 Protein — Easier to Digest

A2 milk contains only the A2 beta-casein protein. Unlike A1 milk (most packaged brands), A2 doesn't produce BCM-7 — the peptide linked to bloating, discomfort, and inflammation.

👶

Better for Children

Growing children who struggle with regular milk often find A2 far easier on their system. Closer in composition to human milk — the way milk was always meant to be consumed.

🌿

Rich in Natural Nutrients

Our grass-fed desi cows produce milk naturally higher in Omega-3s, CLA, and fat-soluble vitamins. No supplements added — it comes from how the animals live.

🔬

Tested Every Batch

We test fat content, SNF, and adulteration before every delivery. Conscious eating means knowing exactly what you're putting in your body — and we make sure you can.
