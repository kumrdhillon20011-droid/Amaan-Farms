How It Works

Farm to Your Doorstep — Every Morning

Simple, transparent, and reliable. Here's exactly how your milk travels from our farm to you.

1

4 AM Milking

Our cattle are milked fresh at 4 AM every morning by our family. No stored milk, no mixing.

2

Test & Chill

Every batch is fat-tested with Lactoscan and chilled to 4°C within 30 minutes of milking.

3

Sealed & Labelled

Poured into clean HDPE glass bottles with tamper-proof seals, batch date, and fat percentage label.

4

At Your Door by 7 AM

Delivered in insulated cold boxes to your colony by 7 AM. Same morning, same freshness.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Amaan Farms milk special? +
Our milk comes from genuine desi breeds — Sahiwal/Gir cows and Murrah buffalo — raised on our own farm with our own fodder. It's fat-tested every batch, chilled within 30 minutes of milking, and at your door the same morning. No middlemen, no adulterants.
Can I pause or cancel my subscription? +
Yes, absolutely. Just WhatsApp us the dates you want to pause and we'll adjust your billing accordingly. No questions asked, no penalties.
Which areas in Amritsar do you deliver to? +
We currently deliver to most of the colonies on Airport Road, Ranjit Avenue, Green Avenue, Civil Lines, Lawrence Road and Holy City. WhatsApp us to check if your area is covered.

We accept UPI payments via Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm. Subscriptions are paid monthly in advance. You'll receive a payment reminder on WhatsApp 3 days before due date.
